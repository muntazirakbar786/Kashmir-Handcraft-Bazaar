About Kashmir Handcraft Bazaar


The Kashmir Handicraft Bazaar is a business firm that deals with the export of kashmir handcrafted products made in srinagar that are exported all over india through courier shipping.Fayaz Ahmad wani from jenab sahib anchar soura srinagar is the owner of the busniness firm. The Kashmir Handcraft firm deals with kani shawl, pashmina shawl, phiran the tradition of kashmir, kashmiri kaftaans, kashmiri silk dresses. kashmiri coats with ari work and etc etc. All of the items are listed in the website.Stay tuned with us on the website and do follow us on instagram and facebook for daily updates.
